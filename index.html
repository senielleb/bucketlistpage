MY BUCKET LIST
I have so many things I want to do in my life—goals to reach, dreams to achieve, and experiences to cherish. From exploring new places to achieving personal milestones, I want to make the most out of every opportunity. Life is short, and I want to live it to the fullest, trying new things, learning, and growing along the way. Here is my bucket list—a collection of dreams and goals that I hope to accomplish one day.
In Life
  • Travel to at least 10 countries – I want to experience different cultures, taste new food, and explore places I’ve only seen in pictures.
  • See the Northern Lights – Watching those magical colors dance in the sky is something I need to witness at least once.
  • Try skydiving or bungee jumping – I want to know what it feels like to free-fall from the sky and conquer my fears.
  • Live in another country for a while – Experiencing a different way of life and adapting to a new culture would be an incredible challenge.
  • Achieve a fitness goal – Whether it’s running a marathon, hiking a difficult trail, or just getting stronger, I want to push myself.
Career and Education
  • Master a programming language – Whether it's Python, Java, or C++, I want to be fluent in at least one.
  • Get a globally recognized IT certification – Like AWS, Cisco, CompTIA, or Google Cloud.
  • Land a remote IT job – Experience the flexibility of working from anywhere.
  • Work for a top tech company – Google, Microsoft, Apple, or any innovative company.
  • Keep learning and evolving in the IT field – Stay updated with new trends, technologies, and advancements.
Relationships
  • Strengthen my bond with my family – Spend more quality time with my parents and siblings.
  • Make lifelong friendships – Build strong connections with people who truly matter.
  • Find love that feels like home – Be with someone who understands and accepts me completely.
  • Always choose love and kindness – Treat every relationship with respect, care, and sincerity.
Financial
  • Build multiple streams of income – Earn through side hustles, freelancing, or passive income sources.
  • Achieve financial independence – Earn enough passive income to support my lifestyle without working full-time.
  • Live below my means but enjoy life – Find a balance between saving and enjoying what I earn.
  • Retire early and enjoy life to the fullest – Work towards a future where I can stop working on my own terms.
Entertainment
  • Go to every concert of my favorite artists – Experience live performances and sing my heart out.
  • Attend a music festival – Enjoy a weekend filled with great music, food, and unforgettable vibes.
  • Attend a fan convention – Meet fellow fans and celebrate my favorite movies, anime, or games.
  • Attend a cultural festival – Celebrate traditions, performances, and local cuisine.
  • Meet a celebrity or artist I admire – A dream moment with someone who inspires me.
